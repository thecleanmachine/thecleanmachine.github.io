Sasank Reddy: Research

My area of research focus was Participatory Sensing. It focuses on developing technological advances for using embedded and mobile sensing in order to enhance civic life and invigorate public space.

Basically, we are looking at using cell phones as “sensors” for purposes such as urban planning, public health, cultural identity, an natural resource management.

To find out more about my research, check out some of my papers.
